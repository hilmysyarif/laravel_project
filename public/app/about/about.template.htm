About INTERTEC

PT. Internasional Teknokom Ciptakreasi (Intertec) has set a firm footing in the Indonesian market for Information and Communication Technology (ICT) & IT related products and services. Through our commitment and dedication, we have received many accolades and recognition both from our suppliers and customers. Today, we have worked with numerous MNCs, including all of the telecommunication providers in Indone- sia, major retailers and electronic marts, IT distributors, and their retail stores. Our distribution network spans wide across the country, penetrating to all market levels.
